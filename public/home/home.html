What is Monthly Roamer?



We are a program that provides the opportunitey for LDS singles to live remotely while working. A group of individuals who want to pursue their love of life through adventure, culture, and service, all while doing their 9-5 with other like minded-individuals.

ADVENTURE

Bring adventure to your life by living in a new place for a month. So many adventures wait in your new monthly roamer life. See, explore, do.

CULTURE

Experience new culutures in a new land. Gain an appreciation for the new and inviting cultures of other countries.

SERVICE

Give back. Becoming a monthly roamer will include service opportunities to give back to the communites we live in.
