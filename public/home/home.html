What is Monthly Roamer?



Montlhy Roamers is a program for LDS singles that would like to work remotely for a month, meet new people, and take a step away from the regular 9-5 routine. A group of individuals who want to pursue their love of life through adventure, culture, and service.

ADVENTURE

Bring adventure to your life by living in a new place for a month. So many adventures await you. See, explore, do.

CULTURE

Experience new culutures in a new land. Gain an appreciation for the new and inviting cultures of other countries.

SERVICE

Becoming a monthly roamer will include service opportunities to give back to the communites we live in.

Come join us for a month...

We invite location-independent professionals to join us who want to live with like-minded people, with like-minded values. This includes entrepreneurs, writers, desingers, developers, teachers, laywers, marketers, photographers, and more.

Through Networking with other monthly roamers, serving in the community, and exploring your new home, you will grow both personally and professionally. We invite you to take the step and join us for a month abroad.
