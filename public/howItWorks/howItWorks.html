How IT Works

Monthly Raomers brings together a group of 20-30 LDS individuals who are able to do their job anywhere. For one month, we will live in another country, experience new food and culture, and also get our work done.

To become a monthly roamer, each individual will fill out an application and have an interview. To start your process - fill out our application now.

Take a look at what's included

Accomadations

Private rooms for anyone that wants one. We also have the option of shared rooms if you would like to save a little money.

Workspace

Working remotely we have a local dedicated co-working space to work from. Good reliable internet is important.

Events

Two outtings planned for the whole group, ex: kayaking tour, rafting, ziplining, etc. Plus activities, firesides, meals, and a whole lot more.

Transportaion

Transportation included to and from the airport for when you come and when you leave. Transportation to the two monthly events.

What do I need to do?

Follow the steps below to get started

Step 1

Fill out our application online. If you are able to work remotely or would like to be considered for our monthly roamer experience apply today.

Still thinking about it, fill out your name and email below and receive updates.

Step 2

We will review your applications and set up a time to do a video interview. We want to get to know you better, and this is our way of meeting you face-to-face.

Video interviews will last about 30 minutes and will help us make a final decision.

Step 3

Get an acceptance email. If you are accepted, pack your bags and get excited to join us for a month of work and adventure.

We will then work with you on all the details.
