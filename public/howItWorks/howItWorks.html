How IT Works

Monthly Raomers brings together a group of 20-30 LDS individuals who are able to do their job anywhere. For one month, we will live in another country, experience new food and culture, and also get our work done.

To become a monthly roamer, each individual will fill out an application and have an interview. To start your process - fill out our application now.

Take a look at what's included

Accomadations

Private rooms for anyone that wants one. We also have the option of shared rooms if you would like to save a little money. We want you to be the one who chooses a roommate or not, not us.

Workspace

Working remotely we have a dedicated co-working space to work from. Good reliable internet is important. So we have contracted with a local co-working or office space to provide you the internet you need to get your work done.

Events

We love getting out to explore. Part of the fun is exploring and adventuring. We have two events/activities planned for the whole group. These are full day events seeing new areas together.

Transportaion

We provide transportation to and from the airport as you are coming and when you leave. Transportation to the two monthly events.
