FAQ

How does it work?

We take a group of 20-30 LDS singles who are able work anywhere. For one month we travel to a chosen destination and work for 4 weeks and explore the country together in our free time. For those interested in joining, fill out an application, we will review your application and let you know if your are accepted.

Im accepted, now what?

We have found a great place to live for the next month. We will have a place for you to work, live, and make new friends. Everyone that is accepted will have their own work to do. From freelance work to starting a business, or your regular 9-5 work that you can do remotely. All information will be sent to you, so pack your bags and get ready for a great month of work and adventure.

What are the accommodations like?

Each country will vary some but we will offer private and shared rooms in each location. We will always provide these options in a house, apartment, or hotel. If you would like a shared room, when you apply, just leave a comment that you would like a shared room and if you know someone else coming please list their name.

Do I need visas?

Visa requirements are left to each individual. For U.S. citizens you can go to travel.state.gov for more information. If you end up needing a visa to enter a country, you will be responsible for obtaining it. Each individual should have a passport that expires 6 months after leaving to return home from their monthly roamer trip.

What do I do about health and travel insurance?

We encourage each individual to reach out to their health insurance provider to see what options they provide for international travel. Its up to you if you decide to keep your domestic insurance or upgrade to an international option. We do advise you to contact your insurance provider about being covered for your trip.

Travel insurance is recommended incase anything happens to your belongings. Its up to you if you want to purchase travel insurance.

What are the prices?

Private rooms are $2000/month.

Shared rooms are $1500/month.

If you are coming with a friend and want to room together, request this on the application in the comments section.

What are the standards?

LDS standards. We will follow the Word of wisdom and law of chastity. We want this to be a great experience for everyone. A place to meet like minded people while living life to the fullest.

Do I need to be a memeber of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints?

We are a group of LDS singles that travel together. At this time we are accepting memebers of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. If you have an LDS friend that is coming that would like you to come please email us at monthlyroamers@gmail.com. We would be happy to review your situation.

Other questions?

Please email us at monthlyroamers@gmail.com. We would be happy to answer any additional questions you have.
