Itinerary

A typical day with Monthly Roamers is up to you but we want to give you an idea of how we spend the day. Get work done and take time to brainstorm new ideas with your fellow monthly roamers.

Our community is a network of devlelopers, marketers, designers, photographers, teachers, yoga instructors, etc. What a great way to open doors to new possibilities.

Typical Day

    6:30-8:00 am: Wake up.
    8:00 am: Breakfast.
    9:00 am: Head to co-working space.
    9:00-12:00 am: Get your work done.
    12:00-1:00 pm: Grab lunch with the group or on your own.
    1:00-5:00 pm: Afternoon of work. If your work is flexible, go explore.
    5:00 pm: Head back to your living space.
    5:00-10:00 pm: Get out and explore, eat, surf, museums, etc.
    6:00-8:00 pm: Grab dinner with a few others or eat in.
    9:00-11:00 pm: Hangout, relax, watch a movie.
    11:00 pm Stay up or head to bed.
